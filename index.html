Winner of 2009 Rotaract Outstanding Project in South Asia
Rotaract Club, a popular youth organization committed for a better tomorrow. Rotaract Club of Jayanagar is popularly known as RCJ was chartered on April 24, 1977
RCJ club has 35 dedicated rotaractors. RCJ has been highly successful in comparison with other Rotaract Clubs around Bangalore. Our past projects have been a concentrated effort to help and focus on the betterment of the society.

A network of alumni in diverse fields have made us aware of the powerful platform that we are on. We have always been in the forefront of Rotaract World.
It gives us tremendous satisfaction on seeing the smiles on the faces of the disabled students, when we help them get an experience of the actual world by celebrating projects like AARAMBH - Sports meet for the Specially Abled.
Similarly, our projects like blood donation, pulse polio camps, teaching under-privileged children, celebration of christmas with children of orphanage were done with the prime focus on society development and also help us Rotaractors to be a better citizen of tomorrow and honing our skills, such activities develop us into complete individuals.
It has by far been one of the most exciting and admired clubs in Bangalore city. We have set standards for ourselves and now we are constantly revising them and upgrading them. This is where we enjoy rotaracting, challenging ourselves, stepping ahead and moving forward - always.
Our Club is proudly sponsored by Rotary Club of Bangalore, Jayanagar. R.I. Dist. 3190
