Mission, Vision and Values  
No Organisation can survive without a great vision, mission and value system. Vision is what drives us to move forward. The missions and values are what we stand by.
Vision
Our Vision is to be the premier Rotaract Club in the World as recognised by Rotary International, citizens, Rotaractors and achieve a formidable position so as to enable us to provide more service to humanity.
Mission
  • To develop the best of the leaders using the non-conventional training simulations.
  • To carry the legacy of Rotaract and constantly empower our parent Rotary Club to achieve its own goals.
  • To be the best Rotaract Club in town with a great blend of learnings, community service and fun.
Values
  • Integrity
    We will always uphold the highest standards of integrity, respect and trust
  • Leadership
    We will encourage our people to become leaders within our club.
  • People
    We will develop, retain and reward exceptional and enthusiastic people.
  • Teamwork
    We will work together in everything we do.
  • Innovation
    We will continually improve our performance through smarter and more creative ways
  • Relationships
    We will build long term relationships and treat each relationship with care.
