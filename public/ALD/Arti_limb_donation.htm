Disha - For A Better Tomorrow...

10 million people in India are suffering from loco motor disabilities out of the total population of 1 Billion. Fitment of Artificial limbs and calipers therefore has to be further augmented on large-scale. This will entail tremendous financial cost. We, appeal to you for generous financial assistance as your moral involvement in this humanitarian work.

We have associated with Sri Bhagavan Mahavir Vikalanga Sahayatha Samithi who donates artificial legs to people that need one but cannot afford one.
