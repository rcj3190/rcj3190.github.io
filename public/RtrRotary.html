Rotary International

Rotary International is a worldwide service organization for leading business and professional men and women with 29,000 Rotary clubs and over 1.2 million members.
Each Rotaract club is sponsored by a local Rotary club. This sponsorship is a result of Rotary's concern that young people, or "New Generations," should take an active interest in community life and have the opportunity for professional development. Rotaract provides a vehicle through which New Generations can find that involvement.

Role of Rotary International
As the apex body, RI administers all the Clubs across the world including Rotary, Rotaract, Interact, Kinderact and Innerwheel. It also sets out goals and broad areas of service for the year and plans out activities towards the same.
RI has been the single most effective voluntary organisation across the world with millions of members affiliated to it.
RI guides the clubs from time to time and even funds the initiatives of the various clubs.
